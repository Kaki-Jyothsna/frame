Home Section

redBus is India’s most trusted bus ticket booking platform. The platform offers an easy-to-use online bus booking service with over 36 million satisfied customers. Partnering with over 3500+ bus operators, redBus offers affordable prices and various bus types to choose from. Also, provides secure payment options and exclusive offers for a smooth and convenient booking experience. Planning a trip with RTC, Government bus operators like APSRTC, TGSRTC, KSRTC (Kerala), RSRTC or private operators like VRL and Orange Travels? redBus offers easy access to lakhs of routes and a wide range of bus types such as AC or Non-AC, Sleeper, Seater, Volvo and more. With a wide range of bus options and services, redBus ensures a reliable and comfortable journey for every passenger.

Services Section

1. Online Bus Ticket Booking:
redBus provides a user-friendly platform for booking bus tickets across a vast network of routes and operators. Users can search for buses based on origin, destination, travel date, and other criteria like bus type, price, and rating. They offer multiple payment options for completing the booking.
2. Cancellation and Rescheduling:
Free Cancellation:
redBus offers free cancellation of bus tickets with a full refund up to a certain time before departure (usually 6 hours).
FlexiTicket:
Users can modify their travel date for a 50% refund up to 12 hours before departure.
Reschedule:
The FlexiTicket option allows users to change their travel date up to 8 hours before departure without any additional cost.
3. Safety and Security:
Primo Services:
redBus partners with top-rated bus operators known for punctuality and customer-friendly service.
Assurance Program:
This program protects travelers against bus operator cancellations by crediting up to INR 500 to their redBus wallet.
24/7 Customer Support:
redBus provides round-the-clock customer service for any assistance related to bookings.
Instant Refunds:
redBus offers instant refunds for cancellation or booking-related issues.
4.Additional Benefits:
Live Bus Tracking: Users can track their bus in real-time to plan their journey more effectively.
Exclusive Deals for Women: redBus offers special deals and priority services for women travelers.
Rewards Program: Users can earn rewards by referring friends to redBus.
5. redRail by redBus:
This service is an authorized partner for train ticket bookings through IRCTC. It includes features like Tatkal booking, PNR status checks, and live train tracking. redRail also offers free cancellation and zero convenience fees.
6. Hotel Booking on redBus:
redBus also allows users to book hotels through their platform, with options for budget hotels and verified customer reviews.

About Section

redBus is India’s largest online bus ticketing platform that has transformed bus travel in the country by bringing ease and convenience to millions of Indians who travel using buses. Founded in 2006, redBus is part of India’s leading online travel company MakeMyTrip Limited (NASDAQ: MMYT). By providing widest choice, superior customer service, lowest prices and unmatched benefits, redBus has served over 18 million customers. redBus has a global presence with operations across Indonesia, Singapore, Malaysia, Colombia and Peru apart from India.
Management Team:
